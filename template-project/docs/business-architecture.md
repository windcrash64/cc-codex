# Business Architecture Snapshot

## Segments
- Creators: AI consultants, prompt engineers, automation agencies.
- Buyers: SMB ops teams, growth teams, and enterprise innovation leads.
- Partners: ecosystem vendors and channel communities.

## Revenue Model
- 15% default take-rate on marketplace transactions.
- Subscriptions: Free, Pro ($39), Team ($149), Enterprise (custom).
- Add-ons: promoted placements, verification, analytics seats.

## Trust Model
- Creator verification (identity + payment method).
- Asset quality score (adoption, refund ratio, SLA adherence).
- Moderation pipeline for abuse and policy violations.

## Flywheel
1. Recruit high-quality creators.
2. Publish trusted assets with proof and ratings.
3. Increase buyer outcomes and retention.
4. Reinvest demand signal into creator incentives.
